Sale Price Extended

On Clicking "Confirm" Button in the Sale Order, If there are Any Updates in the Order Line Price than a Wizard will Pop up asking for the Selection of the Products for which the Prices Should be Updated in the Customer PriceList.On Clicking "Yes" button the Prices will be updated in the Customer Specific Pricelist. After Updating the Price the Order Will Move to Confirm State

Updated Pricelist

The Customer Pricelist with the Updated Prices

Pricelist Configuration

If the Boolean "Do Not Update" is Marked True, than for Such Customer Pricelist, the Routine Odoo Flow Will be Followed, there will be no update on the Customer Pricelist Price
